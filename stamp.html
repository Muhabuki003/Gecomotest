Questions, answered.

Will it work on my skin tone / hair color?⌄
The kit comes in two shades — Dark Brown for black, dark brown and cool-toned hair, and Light Brown for blonde, light brown and warm-toned hair. Each compact layers a lighter front and deeper tail shade, so it blends into a wide range of tones.
How long does it last on skin?⌄
All day under normal wear — it's smudge-resistant and sweat-resistant. It removes easily with any makeup remover or cleansing oil.
How many uses per compact?⌄
Roughly 3–4 months of daily use. The stamps are washable and reusable indefinitely.
What if I stamp it crooked?⌄
Wipe with a damp cotton swab and re-stamp — most people are perfectly even by their second try. And you're covered by the 60-day guarantee either way.
Is it cruelty-free?⌄
Yes — GECOMO products are cruelty-free and the sponges are latex-free.
