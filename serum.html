How it works

Three steps to renewed skin.

STEP 01

Cleanse & dry

Start with clean, dry skin. Pat your face dry with a clean towel — retinol absorbs best on bare skin.

STEP 02

Apply & absorb

Dispense 2–3 drops onto fingertips and gently press into face and neck. Avoid the eye area. Use once daily, preferably at night.

Questions, answered.

Is this safe for sensitive skin?⌄
Yes — our encapsulated retinol is designed for gradual release, which significantly reduces the irritation associated with traditional retinol. We recommend starting with every other night and building up to nightly use. Combined with hyaluronic acid and niacinamide, this formula soothes while it works.
Can I use this with other active ingredients?⌄
For best results, use this serum in your evening routine and avoid layering with other strong actives like AHAs, BHAs, or high-concentration vitamin C on the same night. If you use both, alternate nights. Always follow with moisturizer and morning SPF.
How long until I see results?⌄
Most users notice smoother, more hydrated skin within 1–2 weeks. Fine lines and uneven tone typically show visible improvement around 4 weeks, with continued improvement over 6–8 weeks of consistent use.
Can I use this while pregnant or nursing?⌄
We recommend consulting your healthcare provider before using any retinol product during pregnancy or while nursing. While encapsulated retinol is gentler, it's always best to get medical guidance.
